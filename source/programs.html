The Programs We Offer

Coaching Programs

Online coaching sessions designed to help purpose-driven participants take an existing project from idea to implementation. Sessions meet every week for 5 weeks or every other week for 10 weeks. Limited to 10 teams or individuals.

  • WHO Public entrepreneurs with an identified project on a specific civic tech theme. Maximum of 10 individuals and/or teams per program. By application only.
  • WHAT Intensive, individualized coaching
  • WHEN Every week (for 5 weeks) or every other week (for 10 weeks)
  • WHERE Online

Workshops

Customized 1-3 day, hands on, in-person working sessions for city and government agency-based teams on specific issues and challenges at one or another stage of the governance innovation process.

  • WHO Teams of public entrepreneurs, especially in government or other organizations. Maximum of 24 individuals and/or 6 teams
  • WHAT Highly interactive workshop, leveraging a combination of mini-lectures, team-based exercises, and post-session follow-up
  • WHEN 1-3 day sessions by arrangement
  • WHERE In-person

Master Classes

Masters and professional-level project-based courses and clinics focused on the innovative application of civic technology to public problems. Online or blended delivery.

  • WHO Graduate students and professionals either for credit or for certification
  • WHAT Semester-long, project-based immersion in the all the stages of designing and implementing a civic tech project; structured access to support from subject matter experts, tech experts, mentors, coaches, and peers
  • WHEN Weekly for 5, 12 or 14 weeks
  • WHERE Blended (in person and online)

Courses and Project Clinics

Graduate and professional-level courses, for credit and/or for certification, on the overall governance innovation process, as well as on specific technology-enabled approaches to governance innovation.

  • WHAT Semester-long, case-based immersion on the overall governance innovation process, as well as on specific technology-enabled approaches to governance innovation
